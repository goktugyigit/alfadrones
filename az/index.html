About Us

Pioneering Drone Innovation in Azerbaijan

Our Mission

To lead the evolution of drone-powered solutions in cleaning, inspection, mapping, and agriculture — making urban and natural environments safer, smarter, and more sustainable.

Our Vision

To position Azerbaijan as a regional leader in drone technology and innovation.

We bridge technology and efficiency — redefining how cities, industries, and farmlands are cleaned, inspected, and mapped.
